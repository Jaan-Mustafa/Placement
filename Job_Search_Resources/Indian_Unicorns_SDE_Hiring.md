# Indian Unicorn Startups — SDE Hiring Guide (2025–2026)

> India has **132 unicorns** as of June 2026 — third globally after USA and China. Combined valuation exceeds $392 billion.
> Sources: Tracxn, Inc42, Levels.fyi, 6figr, Glassdoor, Failory, StartupTalky — all cross-verified.

## Salary Benchmarks Across Unicorns

| Level | Typical Range (LPA) | At Top Unicorns |
|---|---|---|
| SDE-1 / Fresher | 12–25 LPA | 18–35 LPA |
| SDE-2 (2–4 yrs) | 20–40 LPA | 30–60 LPA |
| SDE-3 / Senior (5+ yrs) | 40–80 LPA | 60–130 LPA |

> **Note:** CTC = Base + Bonus + ESOPs/RSUs (vesting over 4 years). ESOPs at mid-stage unicorns can add 30–100% on top of base. All figures are approximate.

**Peak hiring windows:** September–November (primary) and January–March.

## Key Insights

1. **Fintech unicorns pay the most** — PhonePe (28 LPA), Razorpay (26 LPA), CRED (29 LPA) for SDE-1. Zepto also competes here at ~24 LPA.
2. **SaaS unicorns** (Postman, BrowserStack, Freshworks) have strong engineering cultures, good WLB, and offer 18–25 LPA for freshers.
3. **BYJU'S** — despite high valuation, is financially distressed. Approach with caution.
4. **Soonicorns** like Whatfix, Cashfree, Atlan, CleverTap are **underrated** — less competition, fast growth, equity upside is real.
5. **Gaming unicorns** (Dream11, MPL, Games24x7) primarily hire experienced engineers; fresher roles are limited.
6. **AI unicorns** (Krutrim, Neysa, Fireflies) are brand new but paying 20–35 LPA — worth targeting early.
7. Apply **within 48 hours** of a job posting — unicorns move fast and close applications quickly.
