## Important Notes

- **CTC = Cost to Company** — includes base salary + bonus + ESOPs/RSUs (vesting over 4 years). In-hand is typically 60–75% of CTC.
- Packages vary by **college tier** (IIT/NIT/Tier-1 vs others). Figures below represent what a **strong candidate** can realistically expect.
- HFT/Quant firm packages almost exclusively go to **IIT candidates** via campus.
- All figures are approximate, sourced from community-verified data (not official).

## Key Insights

1. **HFT/Quant firms** pay the most (50–90 LPA) but recruit almost exclusively from top IITs via campus placement. If you're not from IIT, focus on the FAANG tier.

2. **FAANG India packages** are 35–50 LPA total comp (base + RSU over 4 years + bonus) — not just base salary.

3. **Indian fintech/product startups** (PhonePe 30–36 LPA, Meesho 37 LPA, Dream11 34 LPA) now match global MNCs for strong candidates from any college.

4. **IT services companies** (TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Cognizant) have 10+ LPA tracks — but only through **specialized programs**. The default analyst role pays 3.5–6 LPA. Always apply to the premium/digital track.

5. **Cybersecurity is underrated** — Palo Alto, CrowdStrike, Okta pay 25–55 LPA and have far fewer applicants than typical product companies.

6. **Semiconductor roles** (Qualcomm, TI, TSMC, NVIDIA) pay 20–67 LPA and are great for ECE/CS students with systems knowledge.
